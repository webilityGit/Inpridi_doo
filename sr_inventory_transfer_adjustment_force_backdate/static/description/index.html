Inventory Adjustment And Stock Transfer Force Back Date

This odoo application allows you to transfer stock in back date also you can adjust inventory in back date from inventory Adjustment. It will adapt back date in related stock move and stock accounting entry either you transfer stock or adjust your inventory.
